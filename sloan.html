Description

You went to Sloan, the music center of Davidson's campus. The building was formerly named the Grey College Library and then the Grey College Union, but it underwent a major transformation and is now home to the Music Department. Within the building is the 200-seat Tyler-Tallman Hall, an acoustically and visually stunning recital hall. Sloan also houses practice rooms, classrooms, and much more for students to utilize.
