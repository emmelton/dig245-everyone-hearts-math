Chambers

Fast Facts

  1. LOCATION: chambers building in the center of Davidson's campus.
  2. ADDRESS: 200 D Road; it is not directly off driving-roads but requires walking.
  3. PARKING: not at the building. avaliable along Main Street, Concord Road, or in public parking at Baker Sports Complex.
  4. INSIDE: Classrooms for many non-arts and non-science majors, offices of faculty, and the Registrar's Office.
  5. WORTH A VISIT? the outside.
  6. FAVORITE PART: Chambers lawn that frames the building so well.

Description

You went onto campus to Chambers, the primary classroom building and academic center of campus. Inside, Davidson students attend class, visit and meet with professors, and stop by some administrative offices. The building often has students outside on its front lawn as they sit with friends, do coursework, or hammock.
